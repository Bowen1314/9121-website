Our Mission

Building more than robots

Falcon Force 9121 brings together students, mentors, and industry partners to explore STEM through the FIRST Tech Challenge. From CAD modeling to strategic gameplay, every team member plays a vital role in our success.

Engineering Excellence

We iterate quickly using CAD, rapid prototyping, and rigorous testing to deliver robots that excel on the playing field.

Collaborative Culture

Mentors and students collaborate to solve complex challenges, share knowledge, and cultivate leadership skills.

Community Outreach

We host STEM workshops, mentor younger teams, and inspire future innovators across the Midlands.
